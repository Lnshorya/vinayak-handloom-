    Frequently asked questions
    What is the return policy?

    We have a no questions asked 30-day return policy. You can reach out to us at care@swayamindia.com or WhatsApp on 9999902222.
    When will I get my order?

    We usually dispatch all orders within 1–3 business days. Delivery time varies depending on your location, but most orders are delivered within 5–7 working days across India. You’ll receive tracking
    details via email or SMS once your order is shipped.
    How much does shipping cost?

    Shipping is free for orders above Rs.500.Charges are applicable on Cash on Delivery.
    Are your products made in India?

    Yes, 100%. All Swayam products are conceptualized, designed, and manufactured in our state-of-the-art facilities in Gurugram, India. We take immense pride in supporting Indian artisanship and sustainable, homegrown manufacturing.
    Do you offer customization or bulk orders for gifting or corporate needs?

    Yes, we cater to bulk and corporate gifting requirements with customization options available for select products. For inquiries you can email us at care@swayamindia.com.
    How can I contact customer support?

    You can reach out to our customer care team via email at care@swayamindia.com or Whatsapp us at 9999902222 during working hours (Mon–Sat, 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM). We’re happy to help with any queries related to orders, returns, or product information.
